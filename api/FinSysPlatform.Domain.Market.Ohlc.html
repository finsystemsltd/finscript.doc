Remarks

To save the memory, the time of the tick is represented as a DateTime, without UTC offset (DateTime occupies 8 bytes whereas DateTimeOffset occupies 12 bytes for x32 and 16 bytes for x64, sas well as GlobalDateTime) To get the globally identified time like GlobalDateTime you need a time zone information, usually, it is a parent instrument (TimeZone) or if the instrument is not available, them PrimaryTimeZone
