Remarks

Items in the market depth row collection are grouped by Price: there is a price and attached information about bid and ask volumes and times Pay attention BidTime and AskTime are not the same as Time: they are the time of the last change of the corresponding volume. So, the item may have some price X and different times for last update of Bid and Ask volumes
