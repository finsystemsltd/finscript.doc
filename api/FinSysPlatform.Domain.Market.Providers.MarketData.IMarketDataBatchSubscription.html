Methods

Start()

Enables all subscriptions (Subscribe(InstrumentKey, SubscriptionModel, MarketDataEventHandler)) already made by Subscribe(InstrumentKey, SubscriptionModel, MarketDataEventHandler) Initially batch is stopped. It means that any subscriptions made before is not active until this method is called Also, pay attention that it is highly recommended to subscribe all instruments needed and then call this method. It gives an opportunity to provider to optimize requested subscriptions and execute them at once.

Once started, batch cannot be stopped

void Start()

Stop()

If subscription was started, it stops it. Calling Subscribe(InstrumentKey, SubscriptionModel, MarketDataEventHandler) will be accumulating subscriptions until Start() called

void Stop()

Subscribe(InstrumentKey, SubscriptionModel, MarketDataEventHandler)

Subscribes a handler to market quotes of specified instrumentKey and model Each call generates a new unique subscription key which must be unsubscribed later (see Unsubscribe(params MarketDataSubscriptionKey[]) or Dispose())

Pay attention that any subscription added will really work only after Start() called Also, pay attention that it is highly recommended to subscribe all instruments needed before calling this Start() It gives an opportunity to provider to optimize requested subscriptions and execute them at once
