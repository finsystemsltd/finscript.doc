Remarks

Actually,the index of the item should be inferred from the action: for ItemAdded, LastItemDeleted and LastItemUpdated it is always the last index But, since a series storage supports multi thread access, it may change while the event is being processed, and at the moment of receiving the event, the last index may be different from the one that was at the time of the event This is why the index is passed in the event
