Describes the 3 possible variants for changing the price level command:

  1. Change the level to specified price
  2. Remove the level
  3. Keep the level as is Usually, this type is used with method ChangePositionStopLossTakeProfit(IBrokerageProvider, Position, ChangePositionSlTpParams, ChangePositionSlTpParams)
