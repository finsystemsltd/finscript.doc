Remarks

Pay attention that a simple creation of the indicator does not mean its full initialization For example, a unit may support IScriptEnvironmentAssigner which means the unit needs IScriptEnvironment Also, a unit may support IAccountAssigner and so on Consider using IScriptFactory to create and initialize unit instances
