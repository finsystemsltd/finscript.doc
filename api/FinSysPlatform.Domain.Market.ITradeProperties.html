Remarks

Quantity means the quantity of exit (and the total quantity of the deal accordingly) Profit is a pure profit that calculates as a difference between entry price and exit price It does not include fees (EntryFee, ExitFee) and commissions (EntryCommission, ExitCommission) NetProfit is: Profit + EntryCommission+ExitCommission+EntryFee+ExitFee
