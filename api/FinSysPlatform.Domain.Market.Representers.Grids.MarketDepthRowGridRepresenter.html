Remarks

The market depth rows in MarketDepth are stored separately: bids is a collection of (time, price, volume), and asks are the same - the collection of (time, price, volume) For UI representation, these separate collections have not much sense: the visual "ladder" should be built based on the price levels (the key) and associated volumes and their times (the value) So, due to this requirement, the original data from Asks and Bids is combined into one record using Price as a key, and Volume, Time as an associated data (for volume AskVolume and BidVolume, for time AskTime and BidTime)
